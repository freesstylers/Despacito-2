The Game

Come with Perico in this musical adventure through the depths of Madrid, challenging the best dancers in the capital. Try not to lose your rhythm with our original songs, funny characters and simple but challenging gameplay!

Perico

Murcia's top dancer, he decides to travel to Madrid to achieve his goal of being the very best. Accompanied by his friend and "manager" Marcelino, Perico will face Madrid's elite dancers to achieve his dreams, with a little flirting here and there.

D35P4C1T0

Robot created in 1986 to dance and play the best songs of the decade. However, his age weighs heavily on him, and his style is the basis of what we now know as Vaporwave. Frequents arcade bars in the centre of Madrid.

Papito Daddy

He pretends to be from the Dominican Republic, but Albacete's tastiest daddy doesn't need to disguise his talent. He can be found in Orenses' downs every weekend (or daily, who knows?).

Eminemcia

Born rapper, adopted dancer, this purebred "vallekana" leaves no head unturned with her rhymes. Among her sources of inspiration are Los Chikos del Maíz, Hannah Montana and Camela.

Corpselillo

Died in the 80s due to a sunflower overdose, this zombie from Madrid learned to dance to the rhythm of her grandmother's pacemaker... and SHE'S DEAD. Sometimes his eyes fall out, but it's no problem when he shakes his neck to Rock'n Roll.

Onilecram

The weed alien, the only alien inhabiting the planet. In his first trip to Earth he parked in Siberia, which touched him enough to dance to the sound of his vodka bottle.

The Team

Part-time UCM students, full-time clowns, and programmers in their spare time, the new 9 Horsemen of the Apocalypse, also known as FreeStylers, present their debut title, District Dance Battle, with high doses of humor.
